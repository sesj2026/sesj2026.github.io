Kid's space

• Childcare services (advance registration required), lecture rooms with a kids’ area, and a designated kids’ area (parental supervision required) will be available.

• Children are welcome at the venue
As part of our efforts to support the balance between research activities and family life, participants are welcome to attend the meeting with their children. We kindly ask for your understanding and cooperation to ensure that all participants can attend the meeting comfortably.

[Notes]
• Parents/guardians are responsible for ensuring their children's safety at all times within the venue.
• Please be considerate of presentations and discussions; if necessary, we ask that you temporarily step বাইরে to avoid disruption.
• For information on facilities such as nursing rooms and diaper-changing stations, please refer to the venue guide.
